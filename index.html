About Me

I am Kristen McGraw, a data analyst living in the Metro Atlanta area. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Apparel Merchandising, Design & Production Management from Auburn University, and recently completed Google's Data Analytics Professional Certificate.

I have worked in apparel design, merchandising, and product management for almost five years, and I am now planning to transition into data analytics. Although successful in my career, I have realized the aspects of my work I find most rewarding all center around data-related functions. I am looking forward to the endless possibilies that come from working in the industry.
